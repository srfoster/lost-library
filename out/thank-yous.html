Thank Yous


As a teacher, I find myself eternally grateful to my own teachers. This post is an attempt to say thank you to people I can never repay.

(Disclaimer: If you're one of my former teachers reading this and wondering why I haven't written about you... Remember: I can only express gratitude if I can put what you taught me into words. Sometimes that can take years for me to figure out. It's my failing, not yours.)
